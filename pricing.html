FAQ

Common questions

What counts as a "request"?
Any API call to an AI provider that we track — prompt completions, embeddings, image generations, etc. Batched requests count as individual requests.
Can I change plans anytime?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Prorated billing means you only pay for what you use.
Is there a free trial for Pro?
Yes — 14 days free, no credit card required. Full access to all Pro features.
What happens if I exceed my request limit?
We don't cut you off. You'll get an alert at 80% usage, and we bill overages at $0.0001 per request (Starter) or $0.00005 (Pro). Or upgrade to Enterprise for unlimited.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. If you're not satisfied within 30 days, we'll refund your first month no questions asked.
